The Forgotten Front — Instagram Setup

STEP 1

Switch to Professional Creator Account

This is non-negotiable. Only Professional accounts get access to monetization tools, analytics, contact buttons, and content indexing by Google.
StepAction
1Open Instagram → Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account
2Choose "Creator" (not Business). Creator accounts unlock Reels bonuses, subscriptions, badges, and the creator marketplace. Business accounts have fewer monetization tools.
3Select category: "Education" — this is the most accurate for educational military history content. It appears under your name and signals your niche to both users and the algorithm.
4Turn on Professional Dashboard → ensure Insights is active.
5Go to Settings → Privacy → confirm account is Public (private accounts cannot be indexed or monetized).
6Settings → Privacy → search → ensure "Allow search engines to index your profile" is ON. This lets Google show your posts in search results — doubling your discovery surface.
Pakistan eligibility note: Some Instagram monetization features (Subscriptions, Bonuses, Ad Revenue) are currently limited to specific countries (US, UK, Canada, Japan, South Korea, and others). Pakistan may not be eligible for all in-app monetization features yet. However, brand partnerships, affiliate links, and cross-platform revenue (driving YouTube/KDP sales) work from ANY country. These are your primary Instagram revenue streams initially. Monitor Instagram's monetization eligibility page for expansion to more regions.
STEP 2

Username & Name Field — Instagram SEO Foundation

In 2026, Instagram's search algorithm weights the Name field and Username more heavily than any other profile element. This is where you win or lose discoverability.
FieldWhat to EnterWhy
Username (@handle)@theforgottenfrontConsistent with YouTube (@TheForgottenFront), TikTok, and Twitter/X. Must be identical across all platforms for brand recognition. Lowercase, no dots or underscores.
Name fieldThe Forgotten Front | Military HistoryInstagram's search indexes this field with keyword weighting. "Military History" is the #1 keyword your audience searches for. Anyone searching "military history" on Instagram will find you because it's IN your name field. The pipe character "|" cleanly separates brand name from keyword.
Why "Military History" in the name field matters: Instagram's 2026 "topic cluster" system uses your name field as a primary signal to categorize your account. When someone searches "military history" on Instagram, accounts with that phrase in their name field appear first. Your username alone ("theforgottenfront") tells the algorithm nothing about your topic — the name field does that work. Think of it like Google SEO: your username is the domain name, your name field is the page title.

Recommendation: Option 1. Maximize search discovery first. You can refine the name field after reaching 5K followers when organic discovery matters less than algorithmic recommendation.

STEP 4

Profile Picture — Brand Recognition at 32 Pixels

SpecificationRequirement
ImageThe Forgotten Front logo from Y1.3 — Logo Concept #2 (the badge/emblem). The same one used on YouTube.
Size320 × 320px minimum. Instagram crops to circle — ensure the logo is centered with padding inside the circle crop.
BackgroundDark (#0A0A0A or #1C1917) — matches your brand's dark aesthetic and stands out against Instagram's white/light UI.
Visibility testYour profile pic appears at ~32px in comments and story rings. At that size, only a bold shape or letter is visible. Your emblem logo works. A detailed photograph would not.
Cross-platform consistencyMust be IDENTICAL to your YouTube, TikTok, and Twitter/X profile picture. Brand recognition across platforms is how viewers connect your accounts.
If you haven't created the logo yet: Open Canva → 320×320px → dark background (#0A0A0A) → "TFF" in Oswald Bold, amber (#D97706), centered → thin amber border circle. This takes 5 minutes and works as a placeholder until you refine the branding.

Link-in-Bio Strategy — Your Revenue Hub

Instagram allows up to 5 links in your bio. But more links = more decision fatigue = fewer clicks. Start with 1 primary link, expand to 3 maximum.

Launch (Month 1)Single direct link to your YouTube channel: youtube.com/@TheForgottenFront?sub_confirmation=1 — the sub_confirmation parameter auto-opens the subscribe popup. Maximum conversion.Day 1 until you have KDP books live
