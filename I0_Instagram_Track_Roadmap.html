ROADMAP

I1–I3: Three Projects, 12 Subprojects

📱 Project I1: Instagram Foundation & Profile Setup

4 SUBPROJECTS⏱ BUILD TIME: ~3 HRS📅 WHEN: Week 1
Goal: A professional, algorithm-optimized Instagram page ready to grow from day one. Everything a visitor needs to follow, understand your niche, and find your YouTube/KDP links.

I1.1 — Profile Optimization & Bio Engineering

Instagram SEO setup: keyword-rich name field, niche-clear bio (150 chars), optimized profile picture (The Forgotten Front logo from Y1.3), contact button, Professional Creator account setup, category selection, "Your Algorithm" topic alignment. Link-in-bio strategy: Linktree or Hopp with YouTube channel + KDP books + affiliate links. Bio formula: [What you do] + [For whom] + [CTA].

I1.2 — Visual Identity & Feed Aesthetic

Adapting the Y1.3 brand system for Instagram: grid aesthetic strategy (dark archival with amber accents), Reel cover template (consistent thumbnails in feed view), carousel template (branded slide design in Canva), Story highlight covers (5 categories: About, Latest, Facts, Behind Scenes, Books). Grid planning: first 9 posts create the "storefront" visitors see. Color palette translation from YouTube to Instagram square/vertical formats.

I1.3 — Hashtag & Instagram SEO Strategy

3-5 niche hashtags per post (not 30 — the 2026 algo penalizes spam). Branded hashtag: #TheForgottenFront. Hashtag tiers: 2 niche-specific (#MilitaryHistoryFacts, #ForgottenWars), 1 broad (#HistoryNerd), 1 format (#HistoryReels). Alt-text optimization for search discovery. Caption keyword strategy: Instagram's AI reads captions for topic clustering. Google indexing of public posts — SEO value beyond the app.

I1.4 — Cross-Platform Sync System

The workflow connecting YouTube → Instagram → TikTok. Which Shorts become Reels (all of them). How to remove YouTube watermark (export from CapCut directly, not download from YouTube). Scheduling tools: Meta Business Suite (free) for Reels + feed posts. Optimal posting times: Instagram-specific data vs YouTube schedule. Content calendar integration with Y3.2.

🚀 Project I2: Content Engine & Growth System

4 SUBPROJECTS⏱ BUILD TIME: ~4 HRS📅 WHEN: Weeks 1-4
Goal: A repeatable content machine that produces 10+ Instagram posts per week from content you're already making for YouTube, with growth strategies to reach 10K followers in 3-6 months.

I2.1 — Reels Production & Cross-Post Workflow

Step-by-step Reels workflow: export Shorts from CapCut at 1080×1920 → add Instagram-native captions (not YouTube ones) → adjust music for Instagram (Pixabay Music cross-platform safe) → write caption with keywords → schedule in Meta Business Suite. Instagram-specific hooks (first 0.5 seconds more aggressive than YouTube). Reel cover image creation in Canva for consistent grid aesthetic. Batch scheduling: all 5 weekly Reels scheduled in one 20-min session.

I2.2 — Carousel Design System

Canva carousel template: branded slide design (dark background, Oswald titles, archival imagery, amber accents). 5 carousel formats: "5 Things You Didn't Know", "Then vs Now", "Timeline of Events", "Myth vs Reality", "Who Was [Person]?". Each carousel sourced from your YouTube video research — zero additional research needed. 5-8 slides per carousel. Hook slide design (must stop the scroll). CTA final slide: "Follow for more stories they never taught you."

I2.3 — Stories & Community Engagement Playbook

Daily Stories strategy: 2-3 Stories/day mixing behind-the-scenes, polls, quizzes, archival photo shares, and YouTube video teasers. Story highlight organization and maintenance. Interactive sticker usage: polls (30%+ participation = 45% reach boost), quizzes, question boxes, countdowns. DM engagement: auto-responses setup, genuine reply strategy. Comment strategy: first 30 minutes after posting are critical for algorithm signals.

I2.4 — Growth Acceleration Tactics

Instagram-specific growth strategies for a niche educational page: collab posts with other history pages, "Add Yours" sticker chains for history content, engagement pods (ethical version — genuine niche communities), Reddit × Instagram cross-promotion. Follower milestone actions at 500, 1K, 5K, 10K. Handling the 10K follower push for subscription eligibility. Avoiding growth traps: no bought followers, no engagement bait, no follow-for-follow (all penalized in 2026).

💰 Project I3: Instagram Monetization Activation

4 SUBPROJECTS⏱ BUILD TIME: ~3 HRS📅 WHEN: Month 3-6 (when audience exists)
Goal: Turn your Instagram following into actual revenue through multiple streams — brand deals, subscriptions, affiliate commissions, and cross-platform sales funnels to YouTube and KDP.

I3.1 — Brand Partnership & Sponsorship System

Media kit creation (what to include, how to price). Target sponsors for military history niche: book publishers, strategy video games (Paradox, Total War), educational platforms (Curiosity Stream, Brilliant, Masterclass), history subscription boxes, museum partnerships. Outreach templates. Instagram's Creator Marketplace setup. Pricing model: micro-influencer rates for 1K-10K followers. Sponsored post disclosure rules (FTC compliance). Collab post feature for maximum reach on brand content.

I3.2 — Affiliate Marketing Setup

Amazon Associates setup for history book recommendations. Link-in-bio optimization: Linktree with affiliate links to books mentioned in posts. "Swipe up" replacement strategy (link sticker in Stories at any follower count). Which products to promote: books featured in your videos, archival photography collections, history documentary subscriptions, military history board games. Commission tracking and optimization.

I3.3 — Subscription & Exclusive Content Strategy

Instagram Subscriptions setup (requires 10K followers). Subscription pricing strategy ($4.99/mo sweet spot for educational content). What to offer subscribers: early access to YouTube videos, exclusive behind-the-research Stories, subscriber-only polls choosing next video topics, bonus mini-documentaries, extended archival photo collections. Free vs paid content ratio: 80% free (growth engine) / 20% exclusive (monetization).

I3.4 — Cross-Platform Revenue Optimization

The Instagram → YouTube → KDP revenue funnel. How to drive YouTube subscribers from Instagram (specific CTAs, link placement, Story teasers). How to drive KDP sales from Instagram (book reveal posts, "I wrote this for you" Stories, carousel previews of interior pages). Analytics framework: tracking which Instagram content drives the most YouTube views and KDP purchases. Monthly revenue review template combining all 3 tracks.

TIMELINE

Instagram Growth Timeline — 6-Month Projection

Month 1: Foundation (0 → 500 followers)

Profile setup, first 9 grid posts, begin cross-posting all YouTube Shorts as Reels, 2 carousels/week. Join 5-10 history-focused Instagram communities. Heavy engagement in comments of similar pages. Goal: establish topic cluster identity with the algorithm.

Month 2: Momentum (500 → 2K followers)

Consistent Reels driving non-follower reach. First collab posts with other history pages. Affiliate links live in bio. Stories daily with polls and quizzes. The algorithm has identified your niche and is actively recommending your Reels. First brand inquiries may arrive.

Month 3: Acceleration (2K → 5K followers)

Carousel content driving massive saves. Specific Reels starting to go mini-viral (10K-50K views). Brand partnership outreach begins. Revenue from affiliate links: $30-80/mo. Community feels established — regulars in your comments.

Month 4: Authority (5K → 8K followers)

Recognized as a go-to military history page. Brand deals: 1-2/month at $50-200 each. Instagram Gifts generating $20-50/mo. Your Instagram consistently drives 50-100 YouTube subscribers per week. KDP book links getting regular clicks from Stories.

Month 5: Monetization Gate (8K → 10K followers)

Push toward 10K for Subscription eligibility. Increase Reel frequency slightly. Collab posts with larger pages. The 10K milestone unlocks: Subscriptions, Badges, enhanced analytics, and creator marketplace visibility.

Month 6: Full Monetization (10K+ followers)

Subscriptions live ($4.99/mo). Even at 1% conversion = 100 subscribers = $500/mo from subscriptions alone. Combined with brand deals, affiliates, and cross-platform revenue, Instagram contributes $200-600/mo to your total income.
