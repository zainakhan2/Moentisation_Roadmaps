DECISION 1

🏷️ Publishing Identity: Brand Name vs. Real Name vs. Pen Name

This is a strategic decision, not just a creative one. Here's my recommendation based on your multi-niche strategy:

Recommended: One KDP Account → 3 Pen Names (1 per niche)

Why this is optimal for you:

• One KDP account manages everything — you don't need multiple accounts. Your existing KDP account can manage several pen names simultaneously. When you reach the "Author" section in your book setup, simply input your chosen pen name instead of your real name.

• Each pen name builds niche authority — a "running" pen name with 5 running books looks more credible than a random name with 1 running book + 1 dad book + 1 business book

• Separate Author Central pages — Amazon allows authors to manage up to three pen names per Author Central account. That's perfect — you have exactly 3 niches

• Privacy — your real name stays on your KDP account (for payments/taxes) but never appears publicly on books

• No publisher brand name needed — KDP self-published books show the author name, not a publisher name. A "publisher name" adds nothing to buyer perception on Amazon

ACTION

⚡ KDP Account Setup — Step by Step

1
Create your KDP account → Go to kdp.amazon.com and sign in with your existing Amazon account (or create one). This is free and takes 5 minutes.
2
Complete the Tax Interview → This is REQUIRED before you can receive any royalties. Go to KDP dashboard → Account → Tax Information. Since you're in Pakistan, you'll fill out the W-8BEN form (for non-US individuals). You'll need: your full legal name, address, and tax ID or passport number. There's a US-Pakistan tax treaty that may reduce withholding tax on your US royalties — make sure to claim treaty benefits during the interview.
3
Set up bank/payment information → KDP Dashboard → Account → Payment Settings. You can receive payments via: Electronic Fund Transfer (EFT) to your local bank account (cheapest), wire transfer, or check. EFT is recommended. Payments arrive ~60 days after the end of the month in which sales occur (e.g., January sales paid in late March).
4
Set up KDP Advertising account → Go to ads.amazon.com and link it to your KDP account. Add a credit card for ad billing. You won't run ads immediately, but having the account ready means zero friction when you're ready to advertise (Project K4).
5
Author Central setup (AFTER first book is live) → Go to author.amazon.com. You need at least one published book before you can claim an Author Central profile. An Author page allows you to have all of your work on one page and gives you the opportunity to introduce yourself to your readers. Create one Author Central profile per pen name (up to 3 on one account). Add: author photo/avatar, biography, and links.
6
Bookmark your essential URLs:
• kdp.amazon.com — your publishing dashboard
• KDP Royalty Calculator — calculate royalties before publishing
• KDP Cover Calculator — generate exact cover dimensions
• ads.amazon.com — your advertising dashboard
• author.amazon.com — Author Central profiles
• KDP Printing Costs page — reference for pricing decisions
Important for Pakistan-based sellers: KDP supports payments to Pakistan via EFT and wire transfer. Verify your bank accepts international USD transfers. Some Pakistani banks have specific requirements for receiving foreign payments — check with your bank before setting up. Also ensure your bank account name matches your KDP account name exactly.
