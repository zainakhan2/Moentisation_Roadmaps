MINDSET

The Launch Mindset — Read This Before Video #1

Your first 10 videos will NOT be your best work. This is normal. Every successful channel's earliest videos are noticeably worse than their later ones. The purpose of the first 10 is NOT perfection — it's to: (1) build your production muscle memory, (2) feed the algorithm data about your channel, (3) establish upload consistency, and (4) discover which topics YOUR audience actually responds to. Publish beats perfect. Every single time.
ExpectationRealityWhat to Do About It
Video #1 will get lots of viewsLikely 50–500 views in the first week. Algorithm needs data.Post to Reddit, Facebook groups, Quora. External traffic feeds the algorithm's learning.
Production will take 3.5 hoursVideo #1 will take 5–7 hours. By Video #5 you'll be at 3.5 hrs.Block a full day for Video #1. It's an investment in learning the workflow.
Audio/video quality will be professionalIt'll be "good enough." Not Netflix. That's fine.Focus on CONTENT quality (story, facts, hook) over PRODUCTION quality. Story > polish.
You'll gain subscribers quicklyFirst 100 subscribers are the hardest. Could take 2–4 weeks.Every upload + Shorts + community promotion compounds. Don't check stats daily — check weekly.
The 10-video commitment: Before publishing Video #1, commit to producing ALL 10 regardless of performance. If Video #3 gets 30 views, you still publish Video #4. Most channels die because creators quit at Video 5 when they don't see results. The algorithm needs 10–20 videos to understand your channel. Give it that data.
