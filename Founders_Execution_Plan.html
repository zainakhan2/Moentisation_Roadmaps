CONTEXT

5 Scenarios Explained

🔴 Worst Case (10th percentile)

Algorithm ignores you. Low engagement. No viral moments. You hit every obstacle: bad audio quality, topics that don't resonate, posting inconsistently due to life events. You're in the bottom 10% of new channels. But you keep going.

🟡 Base Case (25th percentile)

Average new channel performance. Slow, steady growth. No breakout videos but consistent modest performance. Some Shorts get traction. KDP books sell a few copies per week. Typical first-year creator trajectory.

🔵 Probable Case (50th percentile)

Your content quality + niche positioning + South Asian differentiator work as planned. You're in the top half of new educational channels. A few videos get algorithmic pickup. KDP books establish steady sales. Instagram grows from Reels discovery.

🟢 Optimistic (75th percentile)

One or two videos get picked up by the algorithm and reach 50K+ views. Reddit promotion drives significant early traffic. South Asian diaspora shares your content widely. KDP books rank well in their categories. Instagram Reels hit Explore page.

🟣 Highly Optimistic (90th percentile)

A video goes genuinely viral (500K+ views). A history subreddit or influencer shares your content. KDP books earn a "Bestseller" badge in a niche category. Everything compounds fast. Top 10% of new channels in niche. This is possible but don't plan for it.
